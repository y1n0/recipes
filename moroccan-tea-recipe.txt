Ingredients:
 - water
 - green tea grain
 - sugar
 - mint or wormwood..

Directions:
 1. boil water
 2. in a pot (called berrad), put two or three spoons of tea grains, and add sugar as desired.
 3. poor the boiling water into the pot.
 4. put the pot to a small fire until it boils.
 5. add the mint or the wormwood.
 6. enjoy.
