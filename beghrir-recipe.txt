Ingredients:
 - water
 - flour
 - baking powder
 - baking soda
 - salt

Directions:
 1. put the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a mixer
 2. add cold water and turn the mixer on for a minute or two.
 3. wait 10-15 minutes until the mixture is ready to get baked
 4. heat a pan on the stove, pour in it a bowl of the mixture and
wait for it until it is cooked
